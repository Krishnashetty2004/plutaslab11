The Manifesto

The Case for Building in India

For years, ambitious Indian founders were told: move to SF, join YC, or you're not serious.

What if that's changing?

The best founders we know are building from Bangalore, not Bay Area. They're shipping products to global customers from HSR Layout. They're raising from top VCs without a US address.

The infrastructure exists. The talent exists. What's been missing is the space — a place where builders collide, where companies form, where the only thing that matters is what you ship.

Plutas Lab is that space.

We're not an accelerator. We don't run a curriculum. We don't pretend to teach you how to build — you already know how. We just give you the room, the community, and the deadline.

Some of you will build alone. The solo founder path is real — Zoom, Vercel, Basecamp all started with one person. AI makes this even more possible.

Some of you will find co-founders here. People you'd never meet otherwise. That's what happens when you put builders in a room.

We don't care how you build. We care that you build.

Our bet: In 10 years, some of the best Indian startups will trace back to this room. Not because we taught them anything — because we gave them the space to start.

FAQ

Questions

What exactly is Plutas Lab? +

A community space for founders in Bangalore. We run Builders Night (monthly, 12 hours, open to all via application) and Cohorts (45 days, selected founders, Demo Day at the end). It's not a co-working space, not a hacker house, not an accelerator. It's a space for people who build.

Is this a hacker house or residency? +

No. You don't live here. You come here to work on your startup. Then you go home.

How is this different from an accelerator? +

We don't run a curriculum. There are no lectures or workshops. We just give you space, community, and a deadline. You already know how to build — we're not here to teach you.

Do you take equity? +

Not today. Someday, maybe. For now, we're focused on building the community and proving the model.

How much does it cost? +

Builders Night is free. Cohort is free. We don't make money from founders. We're exploring sponsorships and grants to sustain this.

Can I build solo? +

Absolutely. Solo founding is a valid path — especially now with AI. You can work alone or find collaborators during the cohort. Your call.

Do I need to be in Bangalore? +

Yes. This is fully in-person. Remote doesn't work for what we're building. If you're not in Bangalore, you'll need to be here for the cohort.
